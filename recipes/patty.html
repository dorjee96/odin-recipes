The Nasty Patty

Description

"Why, that's the most diabolical Krabby Patty ever spawned!"

Ingredients

  • 1 krabby patty
  • 1 tablespoon of hot sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of seahorse radish
  • 1 jar of toenail clippings
  • 2 liters of toilet water
  • 1 smelly gym sock

Steps

  1. Remove top bun from patty.

  2. Pour 1 tablespoon of hot sauce on patty.

  3. Pour 1 tablespoon of seahorse radish on patty.

  4. Sprinkle a pinch of toenail clippings on patty.

  5. Soak patty in 2 liters of toilet water, 1 minute for optimal result.

  6. Dry off the patty using the smelly gym sock.

And voila, you have yourself The Nasty Patty.
